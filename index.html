With Love & Code

"With Love & Code," a collaboration between Digital Altar x Lucky Star, bridges technology and emotion in a unique blend of visual art and music. This generative project leverages HTML5 and the p5.js library to bring to life AI-generated imagery and AI-composed music, exploring the formulas of everyday life and the subtleties of emotion that can be conveyed through digital media. With each page load, there is a new invitation to view the patterns of our existence through a lens that celebrates the intersection of artistic visuals and the foundational role of coding. "With Love & Code" encourages an appreciation for the emotional depth and beauty found in both the images we see and the unseen code that shapes them.
